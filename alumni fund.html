Alumni Support Fund


Over the years, our alumni have generously come forward to assist in times of natural disasters, accidents, and other emergencies. From providing aid to flood victims to supporting urgent medical needs, the SVREC community has always shown solidarity and compassion. This fund was initiated by distinguished alumni with the goal of formalizing the process so that timely assistance can be offered to those in need on a continual basis.

SVREC Aid Foundation

The committee’s tenure will last two years, after which new members will be nominated or elected. The committee will meet as needed to decide on the disbursement of funds to ensure that assistance is given to deserving individuals or causes.
